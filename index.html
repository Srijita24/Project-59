About

Everyone has their own favourite activities which they like to perform in their free time. Same as others I also have a hobby, dancing. It is the only thing which helps me to revitalize my body and have a peaceful day. Usually, I perform classical dances. But besides of classical dance, I learn and perform western forms too. Dancing is not just performed by making some hand movements,postures,expressions and postures. It is something which is to be performed from the soul of our heart.It is completely gained by us when we dedicate ourselsves to the dance, understand the meaning of it and perform it with full of patience and hardwork. Dance not only keeps me physically fit and healthy, but it also makes me and my soul mentally relaxed.
